IndiSanté

Healthcare Sans Borders

Bridging healthcare access for Indian students and migrants in Europe — starting with France and India.

The Problem

Indian students and migrants in Europe face challenges in accessing healthcare due to language barriers, cultural disconnects, high costs, and unfamiliar systems.

Our Solution

IndiSanté connects Indian-origin users in France with culturally familiar doctors from India and Europe, providing affordable teleconsultations, digital prescriptions, and medical support in multiple languages.

Revenue Model

B2C – SaaS for Students

Monthly subscription for unlimited consultations, mental health sessions, and access to our digital care library.

B2B2C – Community Partnerships

Partnering with Indian student associations, diaspora groups, and cultural clubs to offer bundled healthcare solutions.

How It Works

1. Book a Consultation

Choose from a curated list of doctors from India or Europe.

2. Get Medical Advice

Access second opinions, digital prescriptions, or test result reviews.

3. Ongoing Support

Receive chronic care management or mental health guidance.

About Us

We’re a team of passionate healthcare and international business professionals, building IndiSanté to ensure every Indian abroad has access to dignified, familiar, and reliable healthcare support — no matter where they are.
