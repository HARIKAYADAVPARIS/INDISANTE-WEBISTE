Who We Are Looking For

We are building a trusted network of medical professionals who are not only clinically excellent but also deeply empathetic and culturally aware.

  • EU-Licensed Professionals: General Practitioners and specialists fully licensed to practice in an EU member state.
  • Bilingual/Multilingual: Fluent in English and at least one Indian language (e.g., Hindi, Punjabi, Tamil, Gujarati, etc.).
  • Culturally Competent: A passion for providing empathetic care and experience working with diverse patient populations.
  • Tech-Savvy: Comfortable using telemedicine platforms and digital tools for patient care.
